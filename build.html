Build

M A R Design combine the highest standards of craftsmanship and finish, to create contemporary luxury. Creating bespoke living solutions which are unique to each client.

Creating new homes from the finest contemporary materials is our passion. By selecting premium quality, we ensure both longevity of use coupled with unrivalled visual impact on completion. All of our projects are executed to perfection by working with our loyal team of exceptional craftsmen, who have the proven expertise to realise your vision.

We endeavour to use materials, construction techniques and technologies which minimise environmental cost.

With over 20 years constructing some of the very best homes in Shropshire, you can trust M A R Design to offer the standards and guidance required to realise your dream.
