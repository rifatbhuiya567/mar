History

M A R Design was conceived in 2004 from a desire to construct only the most exceptional residential properties in Shropshire. With the simple goal, to work to the very highest standards of design, specification, materials, construction and finishes.

We have collaborated with clients throughout the whole process of creating a portfolio of beautiful new homes. Mentoring and supporting throughout the entire journey is part of the creative process at M A R Design. We have assisted with locating ideally suited opportunities for development, finessed designs with architects, made suggestions for expansion and improvement based on our extensive experience, and consistently delivered head-turning results in this specialist development sector.

We are fully equipped to prepare sites for redevelopment, this often involves a degree of demolition, or groundworks in areas with sensitive or challenging access requirements. Over the past 20 years there have been substantial changes in environmental considerations and legalities, which we always respond to with professional expertise and intelligent solutions.

You can entrust MAR Design to take the greatest care with your entire project, from its unique concept to delivery beyond expectations.
